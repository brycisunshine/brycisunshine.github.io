What I Watch

Shows that
wrecked me (in the best way).

I watch TV the way some people read literature — slowly, with opinions, and too much emotional investment in fictional people.

🏠
Comedy
Modern Family
Proof that family is chaotic, hilarious, and the realest thing you'll ever have. Jay Pritchett is one of TV's best written characters — change my mind.
★★★★★ · Will rewatch forever
⚖️
Drama
Suits
Harvey Specter taught me more about sharp thinking and confidence than most real people I've met. The dialogue is genuinely elite-level writing.
★★★★★ · Peak TV writing
🗽
Drama
Gossip Girl
Blair Waldorf is iconic and I will die on that hill. The plotting, the fashion, the drama — unironically great TV.
★★★★☆ · Guilty pleasure
☕
Drama
Gilmore Girls
Coffee, books, fast dialogue, complicated love. Made me want to live in Stars Hollow and drink more coffee permanently.
★★★★★ · Comfort show forever
