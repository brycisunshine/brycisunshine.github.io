About Me

More than just
an engineer.

I'm someone who genuinely can't pick just one thing to be passionate about — and I've stopped trying. By training I'm an electrical engineer at IIT Bombay, deep in the world of circuits, signals, and silicon. But that's only part of the story.

The other part? I'm obsessed with how stories work — in TV shows, in songs, in books, in conversations at odd hours. The same curiosity that pulls me toward a circuit also pulls me toward a character's emotional arc at 2am.

I've done research at IIT Bombay and NUS Singapore, built things from RC planes to chip-level signal equalizers, and learned somewhere in between that the best engineers are the most curious humans. That's what this space is about.

"Figure it out as you go — that's the only plan worth having."
