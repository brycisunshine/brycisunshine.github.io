About Me

More than just
an engineer.

I'm someone who genuinely can't pick just one thing to be passionate about — and I've stopped trying. By training I'm an electrical engineer at IIT Bombay, deep in the world of circuits, signals, and silicon. But that's only part of the story.

The other part? I'm obsessed with how stories work — in TV shows, in books, in conversations at odd hours. The same curiosity that makes me want to understand a circuit also makes me want to understand a character's motivation at 2am.

I've done research at IIT Bombay and NUS Singapore, built things from RC planes to chip-level equalizers, and somewhere in between learned that the best engineers are also the most curious humans. That's what this space is about.

"Figure it out as you go — that's the only plan worth having."
